The Best Movies According To Somo

My top 3 movies of all-time

La La land

La La Land is a visually stunning and emotionally resonant musical that celebrates dreams, love, and the sacrifices they demand. Directed by Damien Chazelle, the film follows Mia (Emma Stone), an aspiring actress, and Sebastian (Ryan Gosling), a passionate jazz musician, as they navigate their ambitions and romance in Los Angeles. The movie blends old-school Hollywood charm with modern storytelling, featuring breathtaking cinematography, vibrant musical numbers, and heartfelt performances.

Jab We Met

Jab We Met is a heartwarming romantic comedy directed by Imtiaz Ali. The film follows Aditya (Shahid Kapoor), a depressed businessman, who meets the lively and talkative Geet (Kareena Kapoor) on a train. Their unexpected journey together leads to self-discovery, love, and personal transformation.
